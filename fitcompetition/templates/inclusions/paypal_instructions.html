1. Log in to your PayPal account.
  2. Click Send Money at the top of the page.
  3. Fill out the form.
    • Enter {{ "PAYPAL_ACCOUNT_EMAIL"|fromSettings }} in the TO field.
    • Specify at least {{ user.balance|positive|currency }} in the AMOUNT field.
    • Select I'm sending money to family or friends.
  4. Continue with the transaction.
